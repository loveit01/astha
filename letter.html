Dear Astha,

Happy Birthday ❤️

I wanted to do something different this year. Anyone can send a birthday text, but I wanted to make something that would stay with you a little longer.

That's why this little website exists.

While making it, I went through old chats, screenshots, calls, memories and moments. Some made me smile. Some made me laugh. And some made my chest feel heavy because of how much they meant to me.

Thank you for every conversation. Thank you for every update. Thank you for every game. Thank you for every good morning and good night. Thank you for all the little moments that slowly became part of my day.

You probably don't realize it, but many ordinary days became special because you were there.

I know life gets busy. People change. Situations change. But some memories remain special no matter how much time passes.

And I'm grateful for every one of ours.

Today isn't about the past though. Today is about you.

I hope this year brings you happiness. I hope it brings you success. I hope it brings you peace. I hope it brings you countless reasons to smile.

I hope all your wishes come true.

And whenever life feels difficult, please remember that you're stronger than you think.

Thank you for being you.

Happy Birthday, Birthday Girl ❤️
— Anki ✨
